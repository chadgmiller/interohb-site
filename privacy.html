Privacy Policy

Your data, clearly explained.

Effective Date: April 2026

InteroHB is a wellness and fitness training app designed to help users practice heartbeat awareness and recovery exercises.

This page explains what information InteroHB may access, how it is used, and how the app is positioned for personal wellness rather than medical use.

Back to Home Learn more

Important

InteroHB is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, monitor, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

Do not use InteroHB to make medical decisions. If you have concerns about your health, heart, or symptoms, consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Information we access

InteroHB may access information from compatible Bluetooth heartbeat devices that you choose to connect to the app.

Heartbeat

Live heartbeat readings

The app may read current heartbeat values from a connected Bluetooth heartbeat device during training sessions.

Session data

Practice results

InteroHB stores session history, insights, and related performance information locally on your device.

Preferences

App settings

The app may store profile details, preferences, and settings needed to support the user experience locally on your device.

How information is used

InteroHB is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store your personal data on external servers. All session data, including heartbeat measurements and performance metrics, is stored locally on your device. Bluetooth is used solely to connect to compatible heartbeat monitors. We do not use your data for tracking, advertising, or profiling.

Local storage

What stays on your device

Heartbeat readings, session history, insights, and app preferences are intended to remain on your device so you can review your progress without sending personal data to external servers.

No hidden tracking

How connections are used

Bluetooth is only used to connect to compatible heartbeat monitors. InteroHB does not use your data for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

Subscriptions and Billing

Subscriptions

Premium features

InteroHB offers optional premium features through auto-renewable subscriptions. Subscription status may be used within the app to determine access to premium features.

Billing

App Store payments

All payments are processed securely by Apple through your App Store account. We do not have access to your full payment details. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your App Store account settings.

Third-Party Services

Data sharing

What we do not use

InteroHB does not use third-party advertising networks. We do not sell or share your personal data.

Future tools

Policy updates

If analytics or crash reporting tools are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Retention and Deletion

Retention

How long data remains

All data is stored locally on your device.

Deletion

How to remove data

You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. If cloud features are introduced in the future, additional data management options will be provided.

Your Rights

Control

Your data choices

You have full control over your data within the app. Since data is stored locally, you can modify or delete it at any time.

Future updates

Account-based features

If future updates introduce account-based features, we will provide clear options for accessing and deleting your data.

Medical disclaimer

InteroHB is a personal wellness and self-awareness training app.

Not medical use

How to understand the app

InteroHB is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, monitor, or prevent any disease or medical condition. It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice or evaluation.

Safety

When to seek help

If you experience concerning symptoms or have questions about your heart or health, consult a qualified healthcare professional rather than relying on the app.

Your choices

You can stop using Bluetooth-connected devices with the app at any time and can remove the app and its locally stored data from your device.
