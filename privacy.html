Privacy and data use

Your data, clearly explained.

InteroHR is a wellness and fitness training app designed to help users practice heart-rate awareness and recovery exercises.

This page explains what information InteroHR may access, how it is used, and how the app is positioned for personal wellness rather than medical use.

Back to Home Learn more

Important

InteroHR is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, monitor, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

Do not use InteroHR to make medical decisions. If you have concerns about your health, heart, or symptoms, consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Information we access

InteroHR may access information from compatible Bluetooth heart-rate devices that you choose to connect to the app.

Heart rate

Live heart-rate readings

The app may read current heart-rate values from a connected Bluetooth heart-rate device during training sessions.

Session data

Practice results

InteroHR may store the sessions you create in the app, including estimates, recovery attempts, and related practice information.

Preferences

App settings

The app may store profile details, preferences, and settings needed to support the user experience.

How information is used

InteroHR uses information to provide the app’s core wellness and training features.

Core app use

Running the experience

  • Running heart-rate awareness sessions
  • Running recovery practice sessions
  • Displaying session results and trends
  • Supporting app features requested by the user
Data principles

What we do not do

  • We do not sell health or fitness data
  • We do not use health or fitness data for advertising
  • We do not position the app as medical monitoring or diagnosis

On-device storage

Session history, profile information, and app settings are intended to remain on your device unless otherwise stated in the app.

Medical disclaimer

InteroHR is a personal wellness and self-awareness training app.

Not medical use

How to understand the app

InteroHR is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, monitor, or prevent any disease or medical condition. It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice or evaluation.

Safety

When to seek help

If you experience concerning symptoms or have questions about your heart or health, consult a qualified healthcare professional rather than relying on the app.

Your choices

You can stop using Bluetooth-connected devices with the app at any time and can remove the app and its locally stored data from your device.
