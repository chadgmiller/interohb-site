Insights

From sessions to self-awareness

InteroHR is designed to make body awareness feel tangible. Each session gives you a small signal. Repeated over time, those signals become a clearer picture of how accurately you perceive your internal state.

That means less guessing, more noticing, and better awareness of how context changes what you feel.

Explore the science

What you build

Calibration: how closely your estimate matches your actual heart rate.
Bias: whether you tend to overestimate or underestimate.
Consistency: whether your awareness holds up across repeated sessions.
Context breadth: whether your skill transfers across rest, stress, movement, caffeine, and more.
