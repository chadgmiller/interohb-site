Train your inner awareness.

InteroHB is a simple practice app for heartbeat awareness. It helps you pause, notice what you feel, and compare that felt sense with readings from a compatible consumer heart rate monitor.

Start with Sense for a quick check-in, use Flow when you want a slower guided session, and review insights when you want to see how your awareness changes over time.

How InteroHB works

InteroHB keeps the loop intentionally simple: sense first, reveal second, reflect after.

Sense

Check in quickly

Pause, count what you feel, and then compare your estimate with the connected monitor reading.

Flow

Slow the pace

Use a longer, quieter session to spend more time noticing subtle changes in your internal state.

Insights

Review the pattern

Look back across sessions to see trends in estimates, confidence, and practice context.

Built for everyday practice

InteroHB is designed for short, repeatable sessions that fit into normal days. The app keeps the focus on noticing, comparing, and learning from your own patterns.

  • Quick sessions: use Sense when you want a brief check-in
  • Longer sessions: use Flow when you want more time with the signal
  • Private history: review your own session trends on your device
  • Simple context: add notes that help explain what was happening around a session
