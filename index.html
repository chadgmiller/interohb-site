Core features

Simple training, meaningful feedback

InteroHR focuses on a small set of reflective tools that help you notice bodily signals, practice regulation, and see gradual improvement over time.

Pulse Check

Estimate before you look

Pause, sense your heartbeat, and make your best estimate before seeing the device reading. This helps train your awareness rather than just reading numbers.

Recovery Challenge

Practice calming your system

Use guided recovery sessions to work on lowering your heart rate through breathing, attention, and calming strategies that you can carry into daily life.

History & Trends

See progress build gradually

Review past sessions and trends so you can notice patterns, reflect on context, and stay motivated by steady improvement.

What you need

Designed around connected heart-rate input

InteroHR is built for use with compatible Bluetooth heart-rate devices. The app does not depend on medical hardware, but it does require a supported device connection for the full training experience.

Connection

Compatible Bluetooth devices

Use a Bluetooth-enabled heart-rate strap or compatible wearable that can share heart-rate data with your phone. This allows InteroHR to compare your estimate with a live device reading during practice.

Positioning

Wellness, not medical use

InteroHR is intended for personal wellness and self-awareness training. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, monitor, or prevent disease, and it should not be used to make medical decisions.

Why it matters

Interoception supports resilience

Interoception is the sense of what is happening inside your body. Training that awareness can support more grounded responses to stress, effort, recovery, and everyday decision-making.

Awareness

Notice internal signals sooner

By practicing heart-rate estimation and reflection, you become more familiar with how physical sensations show up across rest, focus, stress, and recovery.

Regulation

Build usable calming skills

Recovery practice can help you experiment with what settles your body most effectively, so you can use those tools when you need them.
